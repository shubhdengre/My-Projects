About E-Waste Facility Locator

Our Mission

We are dedicated to helping you find convenient and responsible ways to dispose of your electronic waste (e-waste). Our E-Waste Facility Locator is designed to make it easy for you to locate authorized e-waste recycling facilities near you.

How It Works

Our locator tool uses your location or inputted address to provide you with a list of nearby e-waste recycling centers. You can then choose the one that best suits your needs and contribute to a greener environment by recycling your electronics responsibly.
